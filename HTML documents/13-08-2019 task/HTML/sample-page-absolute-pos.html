CAMPAIGN TITLE

Personal Hygiene

SHORT DESCRIPTION

This campaign is indtended to promote health and hygiene raise awareness about good hygiene habits such
as washing hands,protective mask,gloves and adopting safe drinking water practices

PROGRAM PLAN

IMPLEMENTATION PLAN

Introduce the importance of personal health & hygiene

There is a direct correlation between poor person hygiene and illness. For this reason it is critical that textile
workers and other personnel undersatnd and practice good basics habits,including proper handwashing,on a regular basis. The FDA white paper(FDA 1999) cited poor personal hygiene and improper handwashing as third most important cause of illness This report also cited that 93% of outbreaks related to textile handlers
involved sick workers.Problem associated with these outbreaks included poor personal hygiene,poor handwashing,open sores,improper glove use, and eating while on the job.
